SUMMARY OF EMERGENCY/ INCIDENT
VMECC received call from CCR via Walkie Talkie regarding a report from Yard Leader spotted smoke and spark on roller at TR 06 Hillside. Crew deployed to location from VMECC station. Upon arrival, team assessed the situation and initiated cooling by using Hose reel from FRT. After cooling was initiated, ASC requested Yard Leader to stop conveyor operation to open the guarding for better approach. Conveyor line stopped and guarding was opened by team. Cooling stopped for first temperature check, 31°C on Port side roller and 34°C on CCR side roller. Cooling was carried on. Smoke and spark produced due to accumulation of iron ore. After cooling, temperature was rechecked and was at 31°C on both rollers. After discussion with Yard Leader, he will take over for rectifications by Maintenance Team and cleaning of residue iron ore on the ground by Sodexo. Area handed over to Yard Leader. Team proceeds with housekeeping and RTB upon completion.

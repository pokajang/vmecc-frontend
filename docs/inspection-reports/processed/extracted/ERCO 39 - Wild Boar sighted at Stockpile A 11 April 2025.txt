SUMMARY OF EMERGENCY/ INCIDENT
VMECC received a call via Walkie Talkie from CCR regarding a report from VMM Supervisor was sighted Wild boar at the stockpile A area. Crew deployed to location. Upon arrival, OSC initiated assessment and confirmed there was a Wild Boar. OSC plans to repel wild boar using RIV 01, RIV 02 and Security Patrolling team. Team execute to repel and wild boar heading towards in forest area at Zone 6 (near CMD building). OSC informed to Security supervisor to conduct safety announcement at all BPs Contractor Villa. Team proceed to standby at Zone 6 area. Operation continued to tracked wild boar location in Forest area, Zone 6. During patrolling, team was seeing the Wild boar heading towards at CT 15 area and stockpile E area. Wild boar crosses the road and enters into natural pond area. OSC and Team conducted assessment and confirmed no sign of Wild Boar at Yard area. OSC informed operation standdown and Team RTB.

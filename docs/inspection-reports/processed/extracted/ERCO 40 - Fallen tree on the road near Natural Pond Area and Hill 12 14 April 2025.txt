SUMMARY OF EMERGENCY/ INCIDENT
VMECC received a call via telephone from CCR regarding falling tree on the road at Natural Pond, Zone 3 area. Crew deployed to location. Upon arrival, OSC initiated assessment and confirmed there was a falling tree on the road. Team attempted to cut the tree with existing chain saw but lack of capability. As discussed with Yard Leader, team barricade at falling tree area. Further site assessment found there were few more falling tree and area with minor flooding. OSC notified Yard Leader and Team RTB.

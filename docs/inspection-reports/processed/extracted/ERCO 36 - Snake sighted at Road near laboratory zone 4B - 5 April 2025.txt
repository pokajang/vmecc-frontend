SUMMARY OF EMERGENCY/INCIDENT
VMECC received call via Walkie Talkie from CCR regarding a report from Chargeman that a snake was sighted at road near laboratory. Crew deployed from site to the reported location. Upon arrival, team-initiated assessment and confirmed there was a snake (Non venomous Reticulated Python)at road near laboratory. After donning a PPE, team successfully captured the snake and secured by putting inside the snake bag. Team managed to release the snake outside of VMM premise. Team proceeded with housekeeping and RTB.
